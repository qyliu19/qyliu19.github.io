Qingyu Liu currently is a Research Assistant Professor for Electrical and Computer Engineering with Virginia Tech. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Virginia Tech in 2019, advised by Dr. Haibo Zeng. He received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2014. And he received his B.S. degree in Computer Science from Qingdao University in 2011. He spent half a year visiting the Design of Reliable and Efficient Algorithms, Models, and Systems (DREAMS) Lab in the Department of Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2017, advised by Dr. Minghua Chen. He was a Postdoc for Electrical and Computer Engineering with Virginia Tech from 2019 to 2021, supervised by Dr. Tom Hou. His research interests include wireless and mobile networks, 5G/next-G, CPS/IoT, intelligent transportation, optimization and algorithm design in networked systems.
